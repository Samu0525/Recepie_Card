    1.Gulab Jamun

    Ingredients

    1. Bread
    2. Milk
    3. Sugar
    4. Oil/Ghee

    Recepie

    1. Syrup: Boil 1 cup sugar + 1 cup water. Add 1/2 tsp cardamom powder. Simmer 5-7 mins. Keep warm.
    2. Dough: Remove crusts from 8 bread slices. Tear into pieces. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough.
    3. Balls: Roll dough into smooth balls.
    4. Fry: Heat oil/ghee on medium, then reduce to low. Fry balls until golden brown. Drain.
    5. Soak: Soak fried balls in warm syrup for 30 mins.
    6. Serve: Garnish with nuts. Serve warm or chilled.

    2.Barfii

    Ingredients

    1. 2 cups milk powder
    2. 1 cup condensed milk
    3. 1/4 cup milk
    4. 1/4 cup ghee
    5. 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
    6. 2 tbsp chopped nuts

    Recepie:

    1. Heat Ghee: Melt 1/4 cup ghee.
    2. Mix: Add 1/4 cup milk and 1 cup condensed milk. Stir.
    3. Add Milk Powder: Gradually add 2 cups milk powder, stirring.
    4. Cook: Cook until thick, add 1/2 tsp cardamom powder.
    5. Set: Pour into greased pan, garnish with nuts.
    6. Cool: Refrigerate 1-2 hours, cut and serve.

    3. Kaju Katli

    Ingredients

    1. 1 cup cashews
    2. 1/2 cup sugar
    3. 1/4 cup water
    4. 1 tablespoon ghee (optional)
    5. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)

    Recepie

    1. *Grind* 1 cup cashews into a fine powder.
    2. *Make syrup*: Heat 1/2 cup sugar and 1/4 cup water until it reaches a one-string consistency.
    3. *Add cashew powder* and mix well.
    4. *Cook* on low heat until it forms a dough.
    5. *Roll out* the dough on a greased surface.
    6. *Cut* into diamond shapes.
